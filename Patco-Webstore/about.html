About Us

At Patco Brands, we are passionate about delivering exceptional beverage experiences. As a leader in the development and production of private label products, we pride ourselves on our diverse portfolio, which includes beer, wine, and spirits that cater to a wide range of tastes and preferences. Alongside our well-established national brands, we specialize in creating custom solutions for partners looking to bring their unique beverage visions to life. Whether it’s crafting the perfect blend for a private label spirit or innovating in the beer and wine categories, we bring decades of expertise, quality, and craftsmanship to every product we produce. Our commitment to quality and innovation is at the heart of everything we do. From the first idea to the final product on shelves, we work closely with our partners to ensure every detail is meticulously crafted and meets the highest standards. Join us on a journey of taste and discovery, where we continue to push the boundaries of what’s possible in the world of beverages.
